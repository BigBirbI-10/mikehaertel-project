## The FareScout Origin Story

### The Problem Identified
While working Chicago in summer 2025, experienced the limitations of existing tools:
- **Local newsletter approach**: Austin's weekly event digest only covered 7 days
- **Fragmented information**: No centralized source for traveling drivers
- **Limited planning horizon**: Impossible to plan multi-city routes effectively
- **Manual research**: Hours spent aggregating event data from multiple sources

### The Solution Built
FareScout.App addresses the traveling pedicab driver's unique needs:
- **6-month advance visibility**: Plan routes and travel weeks/months ahead
- **Multi-city coverage**: Single platform for all major pedicab markets
- **Comprehensive data**: ALL events, not just curated highlights
- **Mobile-first design**: Access data on-the-go between rides
- **Data quality**: Deduplicated, verified, enriched event information

### Domain Expertise Advantage
Building FareScout as an active driver provides:
- **User empathy**: Deep understanding of actual driver workflows and pain points
- **Feature validation**: Real-world testing during active shifts
- **Market insight**: First-hand knowledge of what data matters (venue size, event type, timing)
- **Credibility**: Product built by drivers, for drivers
