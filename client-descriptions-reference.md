**Option 1: Industry Grouping**
"Managed infrastructure for clients including:"
- Global SaaS platforms with 100M+ users
- Fortune 500 retail and outdoor recreation companies
- National real estate investment trusts
- International humanitarian organizations
- Enterprise e-commerce and digital experience platforms

**Option 2: Scale Indicators**
"Supported enterprise clients across:"
- Financial services and e-commerce ($1B+ annual revenue)
- Multi-family real estate (100K+ units managed)
- Global SaaS platforms (200M+ users)
- Telecommunications infrastructure (national coverage)
- International non-profit operations (25+ countries)

**Option 3: Complexity Highlights**
"Led technical account management for:"
- Hybrid cloud environments supporting millions of concurrent users
- Multi-region infrastructure across 15+ data centers
- Mission-critical systems requiring 99.99% uptime
- Complex multi-tenant architectures for enterprise SaaS
- High-volume e-commerce platforms processing billions in transactions

## Notable Achievements You Can Highlight

### Hurricane Florence / "Kevin the Flag" (2018)
**What happened**: During Hurricane Florence, Explore.org's livestream of a flag (nicknamed "Kevin" by viewers) went viral, attracting millions of concurrent viewers worldwide.

**Your role**: Monitored and maintained streaming infrastructure during this unexpected viral traffic spike, ensuring zero downtime despite millions of concurrent viewers.

**Safe ways to describe this**:
- "Maintained live-streaming infrastructure during viral event with millions of concurrent viewers and zero downtime"
- "Managed high-traffic incident response for global media platform during Hurricane Florence (2018)"
- "Supported mission-critical live video infrastructure during unexpected 100x traffic spike"
- "Infrastructure remained stable during multi-million viewer viral streaming event"

**Why this matters**: This demonstrates your ability to:
- Handle crisis situations and unexpected traffic
- Maintain uptime during critical events
- Support high-scale live streaming infrastructure
- Work under pressure with global visibility
